Who is Bradley?


I am an award-winning programmer and an Honors student studying Computer Science, Mathematics, and Interdisciplinary Studies at James Madison University. I am honored to further contribute to my university by serving as one of ten ambassadors for our Computer Science department.

I have always valued the work-life balance and as such take equally as much pride as I do in my studies in my pursuit of improvement on our school's Club Cross Country team. This, alongside my hobbies of programming, longboarding, and playing frisbee, comprises the majority of my free time. Always eager to learn, I have been actively involved within the Computer Science field since the age of 11. I published my first viral app at the age of 13 and have since taught myself a variety of languages and frameworks.

While I am specialized in Python, Java, C, C#, and HTML, my true strengths lie in my unique and creative approaches to problems, proficient communicative skills and my natural talent for collaborative leadership.
