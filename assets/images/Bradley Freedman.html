Who is Bradley?

Always eager to learn, I have been actively involved within the Computer Science field since the age of 11. While I am specialized in Python, Java, C, C#, and HTML, my true strengths lie in my unique, creative, and innovative approaches to problems as well as my ability to effectively communicate, collaborate, and lead my peers.
